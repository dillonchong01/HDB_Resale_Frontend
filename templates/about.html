🚀 What We Do

Curious what your HDB flat is really worth? Whether you're selling for top dollar or hunting for a fair deal, our Resale Value Predictor delivers instant, data‑backed estimates based on your flat's specifications.

🔬 How It Works

🔍 Data Collection: We source every HDB resale transaction since 2017 from the Housing and Development Board Database

🤖 AI Processing: Our LightGBM regression model uncovers the hidden factors that truly drive prices

🔄 Continuous Updates: Each week, our automated pipeline pulls in new sales data and retrains the model — so your valuations stay sharp

☁️ Cloud Processing: Your flat's details are forwarded to our REST API hosted on Heroku to obtain resale price predictions

🔗 Developer Access: Developers can also integrate directly: call our API here

📊 Key Factors We Analyze

  • 🏠 Flat Type
  • 🏢 Storey Level
  • 📏 Floor Area
  • ⏳ Remaining Lease
  • 🚶‍♂️ Walking Distance to Nearest MRT
  • 🛍️ Proximity to Shopping Malls
  • 🏫 Distance to Primary Schools
  • 📈 Current Resale Price Index
  • 🌳 Estate Maturity
